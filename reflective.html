REFLECTIVE MATERIALS

They ensure traffic safety, equipment safety and personal safety. They can be in form of reflective heat transfer vinyl, reflective tape, reflective yarn, reflective piping, reflective ribbon or reflective safety silk fabric.

01

TRAFFIC SAFETY REFLECTIVE

These are traffic cones and lifesavers that alert motorists and pedestrians of the activities that are occurring ahead and that they should slow down and be careful on the road.

Key Benefits:

Increases visibility of vehicles especially at night

Enhances road safety

Weather resistant because of their durable material

02

PERSONAL SAFETY REFLECTIVE

They are high visibility safety vests, jackets, raincoats, parkas, pants, protective clothing, industrial protection garments, workwear apparel and so on.

Key Benefits:

Increases visibility of personnel especially at night

Weather resistant because of their durable material

Easy installation

Enhances safety of personnel

03

CONSPICUITY MARKING REFLECTIVE

The National Transport and Safety Authority require all motorists to make themselves and commercial vehicles highly visible using chevrons and reflectors. Reflectop conspicuity markings series are strong and rigid with a high performance adhesive that holds securely to vehicles. Markings are available in rools, packaged pieces or kiss-cut pieces on a roll. The easy-release liner makes installatiom quick and user-friendly.
