We charge Ksh. 35,000 Incl. VAT for the complete installations of Isuzu, Hyundai, Nissan, Toyota and Mitsubishi vehicles. For Mercedes and Scania vehicles, we charge Ksh. 25,000 Incl. VAT.

The speed limiter components have a one year warranty period from the time of installation. Once the warranty expires, each faulty component is charged accordingly.

The price depends on the condition of the vehicle at the time of renewal. Kindly contact 0733375799 for further information.

All our digital speed limiters are equipped with online tracking. In addition, we do offer tracking services at a cost of Ksh. 18, 000 incl. VAT for installation and an annual renewal fee of Ksh. 6,000 incl. VAT.

Multiple users can track the same vehicle(s) but only when the owner of the vehicle(s) has permitted it and has informed us of the same.

In case you have forgotten your password, kindly contact our main office number 0733375799 for assistance as soon as possible.

We accept payments made to our Mpesa Buy Goods Till Number, cash and current corporate cheques. However, we do not accept personal cheques.

We accept purchase orders but only upon discussion between the clients and our accounts office.

We accept payments made after tax has been withheld but upon discussion with our accounts office and supply of the company's KRA Pin Certificate.

If any payment is delayed, we will deactivate your vehicle(s) in the system and notify NTSA of the same. In addition, you will be required to return the speed limiter components to the head office.

Our head office is in Industrial Area, along Lunga Lunga Road next to House of Manji.

We have 4 other main branches countrywide located in Mombasa, Kisumu, Eldoret and Nakuru. In addition, we have prescence in Embu, Nyeri, Meru, Kisii, Kericho, Machakos and Thika.

We do not charge for any services offered such as consultations and vehicle checking. However, we do charge for installations of faulty parts that are out of warranty at the time when the vehicle is checked.

We have a variety of products namely: digital speed limiters and fleet management, tracking, securon seat belts, fire extinguishers, chevrons, reflectors, CCTV systems, biometrics machines, alarm systems, first aid kits and life savers. For more information, visit our Products Page.

We offer a number of services such as sending reminders to clients for annual renewals of the speed limiters, servicing of the speed limiters etc.
