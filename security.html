VEHICLE ALARM SYSTEMS

We specialize in installation of car alarms, in an attempt to discourage theft of a vehicle itself, its contents, or both. We install our car alarms that work by emitting high-volume sound, usually a siren, pre-recorded verbal warning, the vehicle's own horn, or a combination thereof when the conditions necessary for triggering are met, as well as by flashing some of the vehicle's lights, and (optionally) notifying the car's owner via a paging system and interrupting various electrical circuits.
