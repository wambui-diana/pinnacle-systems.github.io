CANVAS SOLUTIONS

Pinnacle Systems Ltd also specializes in the sale of canvas products. We have inhouse designers who are specialized in the design, construction, installation and maintenance of high quality canvas. Deploying our innovative thoughts and ideas, we work closely with clients to design products that meet their specifications.

VEHICLE CANVAS PRODUCTS

We stock and sell vehicle covers (vehicle measured and stitched) that are made to measure for different types of vehicles such as military and police vehicles. The materials that we use are ribstops, tarpaulin PVC and clear PVC which are of high quality.
