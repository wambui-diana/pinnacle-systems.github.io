Our Services

As a certified fleet management company, we put all our efforts in providing innovative fleet management solutions. Through implementation of emerging trends in technology, we give the best products and services to our clients based on their needs and requirements.

01

Automotive Solutions

We specialize in installing and maintaining digital speed limiters embedded with a GPS tracking system in line with the NTSA regulations.

02

Reflective Materials

In partnership with Xiamen REFLECTOP Ltd, we sell high-quality reflective materials ranging from vehicle conspicuity marking to traffic and personal safety.

03

Security Solutions

We provide our clients with high-tech security systems, particularly CCTV, biometrics and alarm systems that relay real-time data and support server storage.

04

Road Safety Equipment

Our road safety solutions range from Securon seat belts to powder fire extinguishers and first aid kits that ensure the safety of motorists and pedestrians.

05

Vehicle Canvas

We custom make and install vehicle tarpaulins made of a variety of high-quality fabrics for different types of vehicles in various sectors.
